About

Aiden Bross is a US influencer from Rochester, New York who originally gained popularity from urban exploration content. His past explorations have led him to various abandoned sites, including military operations. Through his work, Aiden documents the history and stories behind forgotten locations, bringing them to light for millions of viewers worldwide.
