Aiden Bross Is A US Influencer From Rochester NY Who Originally Gained Popularity From Urban Exploration Content

His past explorations have led him to various abandoned sites, including military operations. One video showcased the Rochester Riverside Hotel. In December 2023, Bross and a friend ventured into the closed hotel, capturing video footage that revealed extensive damage and vandalism. This footage was later featured in a News10NBC investigation and an interview with Bross was aired.

In April 2023, Bross explored the abandoned Schiller Park Middle School in Buffalo, New York. His TikTok video showcasing the school's eerie atmosphere, with peeling paint, flooded rooms, and graffiti, went viral.  Multiple news sources reported on Bross & viewers noted its resemblance to settings from the Netflix series "Stranger Things."
